CAN'T SEE FROM AFAR

“‘Can’t See from Afar’ is a short African cultural fiction that tells the story of Econa, a young boy who has visions of his own death but remains unaware of the larger dangers ahead. Guided by his brother Luka, a devil in disguise, Econa is led to ancient demons, where he is forced to surrender his soul.”

Kunabenous the fetish priest once told Odochicka that one from her generation would rob Peter and pay Paul but never wanted ''To blow the gaff'' keeping the information so private for safety reasons, Odochicka replied by saying...........
